# Rank and Rent Compliance Guidelines

## Core Rules

### Never Claim Past Projects or Specific Clients
- Frame scenarios as examples of the type of engagement we can handle
- Invite contact to share real references upon request
- Do not fabricate timelines or outcomes

### No Testimonials or Unverifiable Claims
- No testimonials
- No review stars
- No badges without sources
- No claims about specific past performance

### Tax and Legal Disclaimers
- 1031 defers income tax on qualifying real property
- 1031 does not remove transfer or documentary taxes
- Educational content only
- Not tax, legal, or investment advice

### Securities Disclaimers
- If DST or TIC is mentioned, include a securities disclaimer
- We do not sell securities
- We provide introductions to licensed providers only

### Footer Disclosure
Disclosure: This site routes inquiries to our chosen fulfillment partner for 1031 exchange advisory and property identification support.

## Content Standards

### Language Requirements
- Plain English
- No contractions
- No em dashes
- Short sentences
- Technical accuracy

### Location References
- Include city and state abbreviation where required
- Do not make up location specific information without verification
- Research population, employers, and business districts before writing

### Service Descriptions
- Who it is for
- What is included
- Forty five day and one hundred eighty day timing
- Mention Qualified Intermediary and qualified escrow at a high level

### FAQ Requirements
- Include city and state abbreviation in every answer
- Include at least one identification rules question
- Include at least one boot question
